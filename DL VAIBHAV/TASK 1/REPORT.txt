Report

I created my Digital Literacy Awareness infographic using Canva, as it provides easy-to-use templates and design elements suitable for beginners. My design focuses on explaining the meaning of digital literacy and its importance in students’ daily academic and professional life. The infographic includes key sections such as the definition of digital literacy, useful digital tools like Google Docs and Zoom, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette. I used simple icons, minimal text, and a clean layout to make the information easy to understand at a glance.

One interesting aspect of this task was learning how to visually organize information so that it looks attractive while still being informative. I realized that using too much text makes the infographic confusing, so maintaining balance was important. The most difficult part was choosing the right layout and color combination to make the design look professional without overcrowding it. Overall, this activity improved my understanding of digital literacy and enhanced my design skills.
